Hi, I’m Wenhui Zhao.

I’m a PhD student at the USC Leonard Davis School of Gerontology, mentored by Dr. Mara Mather in the Emotion & Cognition Lab. I apply advanced statistics and multimodal neuroimaging to study MRI-based markers of the locus coeruleus, their links to cognitive function and Alzheimer’s disease, and how cognitive training shapes brain structure and function.
