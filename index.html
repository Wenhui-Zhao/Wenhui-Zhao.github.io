PhD Student · USC Leonard Davis School of Gerontology

I’m a PhD student at the USC Leonard Davis School of Gerontology, in Dr. Mather’s Lab. I apply advanced statistics and multimodal neuroimaging to study MRI-based markers of the locus coeruleus, their links to cognitive function and Alzheimer’s disease, and how cognitive training shapes brain structure and function.
